Quick Reference

Hold to Activate

A⌘ LGUI
S⌥ LALT
D⌃ LCTRL
F⇧ LSHIFT
J⇧ RSHIFT
K⌃ RCTRL
L⌥ RALT
;⌘ RGUI
ENT▶ Layer 1 (Symbols)
[L3]▶ Layer 3 (Navigation)
[L2]▶ Layer 2 (Numbers+Fn)

Combos & Special

SPC + ENT → Layer 2 (Numbers+Fn)
CW → Caps Word — ALL_CAPS until space/symbol
BTCLR → Clear current BT pairing
BT0–4 → Switch BT device (L1, top row)
HRM timing: hold >200ms triggers modifier · idle 150ms required
Misfiring? ↑ require-prior-idle-ms (try 200)
Too slow? ↓ tapping-term-ms (try 180)
Nav keys mirror vim: H← J↓ K↑ L→ · Y=HOME U=PGDN I=PGUP O=END
