Where should the keyboard HUD live?

Pick the placement that fits how you work. It'll be always-on-top and draggable.

Bottom Center

Sits along the bottom edge of your screen, centered. Out of the way but always visible. Good if you use your full screen width for work.

Bottom-Right Corner

Tucked in a corner, smaller footprint. Stays out of your main work area. Glanceable without being distracting.

Top Center

Floats near the top of your screen. Easy to glance up at, away from keyboard focus area. Good for multi-monitor setups.

All options are draggable — this is just the default position.
